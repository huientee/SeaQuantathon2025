About

Who are we?

We are Quantum Climate Rangers, a dedicated team passionate about environmental science and quantum machine learning, striving to provide accurate and insightful ocean heat map predictions.

Project

This project aims to leverage quantum machine learning models to forecast ocean heat distribution, aiding in climate research and marine conservation efforts.

Visions

To develop a robust and highly accurate quantum machine learning framework that provides significantly extended accuracy and lead-time forecasts of marine heatwaves in the South China Sea, thereby enabling proactive mitigation strategies to protect marine ecosystems, support coastal communities, and inform regional policy decisions in the face of escalating climate change impacts.

Quantum Seas

This application was developed as part of the SEA Quantathon 2025, an event focused on innovative solutions using quantitative methods and quantum computing.
